How It Works

Insertion sort organizes cards like sorting a hand of playing cards:

  1. Pick up the next unsorted card
  2. Compare it with cards in the sorted hand
  3. Slide larger cards to the right
  4. Insert the card in its correct position
  5. Repeat until all cards are sorted
Time Complexity: O(n²)
Space Complexity: O(1)
